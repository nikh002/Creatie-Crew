Any type of event, diturbance,accident alert will be shared with other users and local authorities or community leaders to verify and manage alerts. What is information alert? Information alerts are a special ipMonitor notification type. Their purpose is to locate and retrieve information within structured data sources that contain variable data. Information alerts are only triggered by the File Watching, Event Log, and SNMP Trap monitors.
